Widths. Active zone flex 3.4, resting zones flex 1, gap 6px. On zone change the flex values animate (spring, ~350 ms) — the bar re-flows, nothing jumps.
Fill. Active segment: 30% orange fill + 45% orange border. Resting: #2e2e2e. Zone 5 active: solid #FF4D00 + glow.
Pointer. 4×22 pt white bar (2 pt radius), overshoots the 12 pt track by 5 pt top/bottom, soft white glow. Position = (bpm − zoneMin) / (zoneMax − zoneMin), clamped 4%–96%, eased over 1 s as HR updates.
No digits. BPM is never shown — position inside the zone is the message.
