CURRIMUS

Privacy Policy

Last updated 24 July 2026

Currimus has no servers. It collects nothing, sends nothing and has no account to sign in to. Everything below is a consequence of that.

What the app stores, and where

Currimus records runs on your Apple Watch and shows them on your iPhone. The data it handles is:

  • Your runs — distance, duration, pace, per-kilometre splits, heart rate and time in heart-rate zones.
  • Your GPS track and elevation for each run, when location access is granted.
  • Your settings — heart-rate zones, weekly goal, pacer defaults, target race.

All of it is written to your device and to Apple Health, which is Apple's own store on your device and under your control. Nothing is transmitted to us or to anyone else, because there is nowhere for it to go: the app makes no network requests.

Apple Health

Currimus asks for two things from Apple Health, and only uses them for what it says:

  • Permission to save workouts, so a finished run becomes a running workout in Health like any other.
  • Permission to read your heart rate, distance, running workouts, resting heart rate and date of birth — to show live pace and zones during a run, to derive your heart-rate zones from measurements instead of a formula, and to count runs you recorded in other apps towards your totals.

Health data is never used for advertising, marketing, data mining, or shared with any third party. It is never sold. It never leaves your device through Currimus.

Location

Location is used while a run is recording, to trace the route and measure climb and elevation. It is used in the background during a run, because your wrist is down for most of one. It is not used at any other time, and a run records distance, pace and heart rate perfectly well without it.

What Currimus does not do

  • No analytics, telemetry, crash reporting or usage tracking.
  • No advertising, and no advertising identifiers.
  • No third-party SDKs of any kind.
  • No accounts, no sign-in, no email address collected.
  • No data sold, shared or disclosed to anyone.

Your data is yours

  • Delete a single run by pressing and holding it in the log.
  • Export everything as GPX and CSV from Settings, at any time, with no account and no lock-in.
  • Deleting the app removes everything Currimus stored on your device. Workouts already saved to Apple Health stay there, and are managed in the Health app — Currimus cannot reach them once it is gone.
  • Revoke Health or location access at any time in iOS Settings.

Children

Currimus is not directed at children under 13, and collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the revised version will be posted here with a new date. Since the app collects nothing, any change is likely to be a clarification rather than a new use of data.
